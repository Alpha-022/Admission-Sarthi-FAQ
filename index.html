FAQs

What is Admission Sarthi?

Admission Sarthi is a portal where you can find your preferred college based on your percentile, access college details, and take mock tests.

Are you affiliated with DTE or CET Cell Maharashtra?

No, we are not affiliated with either of them. Admission Sarthi is a final-year project designed for educational purposes only.

Can I fill the option form based on the list given by Admission Sarthi?

No, you cannot. Admission Sarthi is intended for educational purposes only. While you can use it as a reference, you should conduct your own research about colleges. We are not liable for any consequences resulting from your decisions.

Why are some colleges missing from the list?

Currently, we have included colleges from Pune only. We have not yet decided whether to extend our support to colleges across India.

Will this portal provide support for JEE in the future?

No, Admission Sarthi will only provide support for MHT-CET-based engineering colleges.
